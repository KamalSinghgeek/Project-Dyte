1. **Log into the Admin Panel**:

   - Use your **admin credentials** (username and password) to log in.

2. **Navigate to the Dashboard**:
   - Once logged in, go to the **Dashboard** section.
   - Review essential details like the **total number of vehicles**, **users**, and **payments**.
