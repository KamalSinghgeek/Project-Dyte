# Credit Pricing Guide

## Steps for Managing Credits in Fleet Stack

### 1. Access the Dashboard

- **Log Into the Admin Panel**:
  - Use your credentials to access the admin panel.
- **Navigate to the Dashboard**:
  - Review key details such as the total number of vehicles, users, and payments.

### 2. Request Credits

- Click on the **Request Credit** button in the Dashboard.
- A dialog box will appear to input credit details.

### 3. Enter Credit Details

- **Input the Required Credit Count**:
  - Specify the number of credits you want to request in the dialog box.
- Press the **Request** button to submit the credit request.

### 4. Review Credit Requests

- Monitor the status of your credit requests in the admin panel.
- Keep track of updates and responses as needed.

### 5. Manage Payments

- **Navigate to the Payments Section**:
  - Handle any charges associated with the credit request.
- **Select the Relevant Vehicle or User**:
  - Choose the entity requiring credit or license renewal.
- **Enter Payment Details**:
  - Fill in the amount, payment method, and any reference information.
- **Save the Payment**:
  - Finalize the payment to renew licenses or allocate credits.

This process ensures an efficient workflow for requesting and allocating credits, seamlessly integrating credit management with vehicle or user operations.
