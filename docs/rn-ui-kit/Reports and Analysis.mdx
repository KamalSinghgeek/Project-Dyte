### Reports

- **Accessing the Reports Page**:

  - Select a vehicle and time period.
  - Tap **Show Results** to generate reports.
  - Details include:
    - Total Running Time
    - Kilometers Traveled
    - Total Stoppages
    - Start and End Points

- **Sharing Reports**:
  - Export and share reports via email or messaging.
