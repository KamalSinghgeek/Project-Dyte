# Parking Mode

Parking Mode is a feature that allows you to receive notifications related to the status of your vehicles when they are parked. It helps you keep track of your fleet and ensures that no vehicle is left unattended or at risk.

### **How to Use Parking Mode:**

- **Activate Parking Mode**: Enable parking mode to receive notifications when a vehicle enters or leaves a designated parking area.
- **Deactivate Parking Mode**: Disable parking mode to stop receiving these notifications.

By using Parking Mode, you can stay informed about the status of your fleet and ensure your vehicles are secure when not in use.
