# Notification Settings

The **Notification Settings** section lets you tailor your notification preferences to ensure you receive timely updates and alerts that matter most. From here, you can customize notification types, manage alerts, and ensure you're always in the loop. Access this section directly through the **Settings** menu.

---

### **Accessing Notification Settings**

To begin configuring your notifications, head over to **Settings** in the Fleet Stack app. From there, you'll find **Notification Settings** where all your preferences are managed.

## **Quick Notification Configuration for Fleet Stack Pro App**

Follow these simple steps to quickly configure your notifications in the Fleet Stack Pro app:

1. **Open Settings**:
   - Launch the Fleet Stack app and navigate to **Settings**.

2. **Enter Token**:
   - Copy the provided token (e.g., `d2VpUTd6RvqvJ304UncJYC:APA91bG...`) and paste it in the **Your Token** field.

3. **Configure Notifications**:
   - Tap **Configure Notification** to customize your preferences, such as notification types and frequency.

4. **Save Settings**:
   - After adjusting your settings, save the changes to apply them.

5. **Test Notifications**:
   - Optionally, test the notification to confirm everything is set up correctly.

6. **Done!**
   - You're all set! Your notifications are now configured and ready to go.
