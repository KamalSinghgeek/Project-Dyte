# Navigate

The **Navigate** feature allows you to easily navigate to the current location of your vehicle using **Google Maps**. This is especially useful for fleet managers or drivers who need to quickly get directions to a vehicle's real-time location.

### **How to Navigate:**

- Simply tap on the **Navigate** option, and the map will open your current location on **Google Maps**.
- The app will then provide directions to the selected vehicle's location in real-time.

This feature streamlines route planning and allows for quick access to any vehicle in your fleet, helping you respond faster in any situation.
