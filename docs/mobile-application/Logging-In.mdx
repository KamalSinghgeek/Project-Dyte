# Log In

---

To access the **Fleet Stack Mobile App**, follow these simple steps to log in and begin using the system:

---

### **Steps to Log In**:

1. **Open the Fleet Stack Mobile App**: Launch the app on your mobile device.

2. **Enter Your Login Credentials**: Input your **Username** and **Password**. Ensure the credentials provided to you are correct.

3. **Tap Login**: Once the credentials are entered, tap the **Login** button to proceed. After successful authentication, the app will grant you access to the dashboard, where you can start managing your fleet.

---

This process ensures that only authorized users can access the app, safeguarding your fleet data and information.
