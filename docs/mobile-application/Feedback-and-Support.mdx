# Feedback and Support

---

The **Feedback and Support** section provides users with options to share their experiences and access assistance when needed. These tools ensure that users can communicate their thoughts and receive help promptly.

---

### **Share Feedback**:

This feature allows users to share their suggestions, complaints, or any other feedback related to the Fleet Stack system. Whether it's an idea for improvement or an issue that needs addressing, users can submit their input directly through the application.

- **Submit suggestions or complaints**: Use the provided feedback option to easily share your thoughts with the admin. This helps improve the system and address any issues promptly.

---

### **Access Support**:

The support section provides users with quick access to the help they need. It includes frequently asked questions (FAQs) and direct contact information for admins, ensuring users can find solutions to common issues or reach out for personalized assistance.

- **FAQs**: Access common solutions and troubleshooting tips that address frequently encountered issues.
- **Admin contact information**: Get in touch with the admin directly for further assistance or to escalate unresolved problems.
