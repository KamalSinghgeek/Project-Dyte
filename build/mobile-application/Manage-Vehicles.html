Manage Vehicles

The Manage Vehicles section allows administrators to manage and control vehicle information within the system. This includes editing vehicle details, viewing their current location, and sending specific commands to the vehicle’s devices.

Edit Vehicle Details:​

  • Update vehicle information: You can modify the vehicle’s make, model, registration, or any other relevant details as needed.

Navigate to Vehicle’s Location:​

  • View real-time vehicle location: You can navigate to the vehicle’s current location by selecting it on the map.

Send Commands to Devices:​

  • Send commands to vehicle devices: Admins can send commands to devices installed in the vehicle, such as adjusting the APN (Access Point Name) or operator settings to ensure optimal connectivity.

This section provides administrators with full control over vehicle data, allowing for real-time tracking and the ability to manage device settings remotely.

This feature is designed to help administrators keep track of vehicle status, send necessary commands, and update vehicle details efficiently.
